# Privacy Policy — Insult Sword Fighting

Last updated: TBD-pre-launch.

## Data we collect

- Email (for magic link login)
- Display name (optional)
- Game content you create (insults, replies)
- Match history (challenges, matches, turns)
- IP address, in Cloudflare Workers logs (~7-day retention)

## What we don't do

- We don't track you across other sites.
- We don't use cookies for analytics. Sessions use a strictly necessary cookie.
- We don't sell your data.

## Your rights (GDPR)

- Right to erasure: Profile → Delete account.
- Right to portability: Profile → Export data (JSON).

## Age requirement

Service available to users 16+.

## Logs

Cloudflare Workers logs include IP, retained ~7 days. AI Gateway logs prompt+response text without additional PII.
